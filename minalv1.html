MINAL is a license with multiple options.
Options:
- AT | Attribution...| Other people who share your work MUST give credit to the original creator of the work.
- !C | NoCommercial..| Other people who share your work are not allowed monitization of the content.
- ED | Educational...| Your work can be used for educational purpouses.
- PD | PublicDomain..| Other people can use your work with no restrictions.
- SH | ShareAlike....| Other people who share your work MUST use the same license.
- !D | NoDerivatives.| Other people can share your work but can NOT change it.
