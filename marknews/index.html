Villagers declare war on Pillagers

March 25th, 2001

The Villagers of The Democratic Republic of North Bellisa have declared war on The Pillagers of The Democratic Republic of South Bayesha.
Officials warn that this conflict could escalate into a major war between two giants.
Both sides have begun to mobilize forces along the border.
North Bellisa's border is now very strict to decrease the chance of attacks.


Huge explosion in South Bellisa

March 21st, 2001

A Power Plant of South Bellisa caught fire and caused an explosion.
Officials believe this was because of a coolant leak in sector ███.
The coolant leak stopped flow of coolant to the main reactor which caused a huge explosion.
The real reason is yet to be discovered as an investigation starts.
